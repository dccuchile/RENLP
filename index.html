ReLeLA

The REpresentation LEarning and Natural LAnguage Processing (ReLeLa) research group located within the Department of Computer Science (DCC) at the University of Chile focuses on the intersection of representation learning and natural language processing, as well as other topics related to data science and artificial intelligence.
